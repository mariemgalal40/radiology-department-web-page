MRI


MRI is non-invasive and painless procedure that uses a strong magnetic field and radio waves to create detailed images of the organs and tissues within the body and sometimes to detect clots or tumors.

CT


CT is combination a series of X-ray images taken from different angles around your body that provides a 3D picture of your injury, Using in diagnostic of muscle and bone disorders, Pinpoint the location of a tumor, infection , guide radiation therapy.

Ultrasound


Ultrasound is safe, noninvasive and non ionizing radiation that uses sound waves to diagnose the causes of pain, swelling, infection, the body's internal organs and to examine a baby in pregnant women. It's also used to guide biopsies, diagnose heart conditions.
